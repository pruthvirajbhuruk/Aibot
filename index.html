Empowering Disabled Individuals with AI

An AI-powered assistant designed for those who cannot hear, see, or speak, providing education and guidance.

What is "AI For Disabled People"?

This AI chatbot is designed to assist individuals who are deaf, blind, or mute by serving as an educational instructor and a daily companion.
